# 35 — Reseller Acquisition & Enablement Playbook

**Version:** v1.3  
**Date:** 2026-04-22  
**Scope:** Reseller growth strategy after MVP/pilot readiness.

## 1. Purpose

This document defines how to find, qualify, onboard, and enable resellers. Resellers do not buy dashboards; they buy the ability to monetize their audience without owning infrastructure chaos.

## 2. Ideal reseller profiles

### Hosting reseller

```text
Need: branded VPS/proxy storefront, client billing, service management.
Pain: manual provider/order/support process.
```

### MMO/tool community owner

```text
Need: proxy/VPS supply for existing audience.
Pain: unstable source, chat-based sales, difficult renewals.
```

### SEO/digital agency

```text
Need: infrastructure for client workflows.
Pain: scattered provider billing and support.
```

### Freelancer/consultant

```text
Need: sell managed setup/support around VPS/proxy.
Pain: no portal, no lifecycle management.
```

## 3. Value proposition

```text
Launch your own branded VPS/proxy storefront without owning infrastructure.
Control clients, pricing, wallet, and service lifecycle while the platform handles provider sourcing, provisioning, ledger, and operations backbone.
```

Vietnamese:

```text
Có shop VPS/proxy mang thương hiệu riêng, tự set giá, tự quản khách, không cần tự ôm hạ tầng/provider/API phức tạp.
```

## 4. Qualification checklist

```text
- has existing audience/client base
- understands VPS/proxy market
- accepts prepaid reseller wallet
- follows AUP/abuse policy
- can handle first-level customer communication
- has realistic margin expectation
- no obvious fraud/abuse risk
```

Avoid early:

```text
anonymous high-volume reseller
asks for postpaid immediately
wants abuse-heavy traffic
refuses AUP
only cares about lowest price
```

## 5. Onboarding steps

```text
1. Qualification call/form.
2. Explain prepaid wallet and settlement.
3. Create reseller tenant.
4. Configure brand/logo/domain basics.
5. Clone allowed catalog.
6. Set reseller prices.
7. Top up reseller wallet.
8. Create demo/first client.
9. Place test order.
10. Teach support/renew/top-up flow.
11. Set pilot limits.
12. Review after first week.
```

## 6. Training modules

```text
1. Platform overview
2. Wallet and settlement
3. Catalog and pricing
4. Client top-up/order flow
5. Service lifecycle
6. Abuse policy

```

## 7. Pricing guidance

Commodity product:

```text
Margin: 10–20%
Use: acquisition/volume
Risk: support can eat margin
```

Standard product:

```text
Margin: 20–40%
Use: normal VPS/proxy packages
```

Managed/premium product:

```text
Margin: 40–70%+
Use: setup, support, consulting, private source
```

## 8. Margin formula

```text
Gross profit = selling_price - reseller_cost
Gross margin % = (selling_price - reseller_cost) / selling_price * 100
```

Example:

```text
Selling price: 20 USD
Reseller cost: 12 USD
Gross profit: 8 USD
Gross margin: 40%
```

## 10. Risk flags

```text
many new clients with same pattern
frequent abuse
frequent refunds
payment/top-up issues
selling below cost by mistake
low balance blocking orders
requests to bypass abuse/provider rules
```

Actions:

```text
education
lower limits
manual review
restrict products
suspend tenant if severe
```

## 11. Sales scripts

Opening:

```text
Bạn đang bán VPS/proxy hoặc có tệp khách cần hạ tầng? Bên mình có thể mở cho bạn một storefront mang brand riêng, tự set giá, tự quản khách, dùng ví prepaid, không cần tự tích hợp provider hay xây hệ thống từ đầu.
```

Pain-based:

```text
Nếu bạn đang bán thủ công qua chat, vấn đề lớn không phải là thiếu khách. Vấn đề là càng bán càng rối: tiền, gia hạn, credential, support, nguồn hàng. Platform này gom các flow đó vào một hệ thống có ledger, lifecycle và portal riêng cho reseller.
```

Prepaid explanation:

```text
Reseller wallet là ví settlement giữa bạn và platform. Client của bạn có thể nạp ví nội bộ, nhưng platform chỉ cấp tài nguyên khi reseller wallet đủ cost. Cách này bảo vệ cả hai bên khỏi nợ xấu và order ảo.
```

## 12. FAQ

```text
Q: Tôi có thể tự set giá không?
A: Có, trong giới hạn margin/policy của platform.

Q: Khách của tôi có thấy platform mẹ không?
A: MVP hỗ trợ white-label cơ bản; mức độ phụ thuộc domain/theme/template.

Q: Tại sao phải nạp reseller wallet trước?
A: Vì provider/resource là chi phí thật. Prepaid settlement tránh nợ xấu.

Q: Provider lỗi thì sao?
A: Order đi qua provisioning safety flow; lỗi chắc chắn có thể refund theo policy, lỗi uncertain sẽ manual review.
```
