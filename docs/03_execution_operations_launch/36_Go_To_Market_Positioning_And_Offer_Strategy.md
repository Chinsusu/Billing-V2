# 36 — Go-To-Market Positioning & Offer Strategy

**Version:** v1.3  
**Date:** 2026-04-22  
**Scope:** GTM after MVP/pilot readiness.

## 1. Purpose

This document defines positioning, target customers, offers, messaging, and channels for the white-label VPS/proxy platform. It should not be used to scale acquisition before the core platform passes launch gates.

## 2. Positioning statement

```text
For small hosting sellers, MMO/SEO communities, digital agencies, and infrastructure resellers,
the platform provides a white-label VPS/proxy storefront with wallet billing, reseller pricing, service lifecycle, and automated/manual provisioning,
so they can sell infrastructure under their own brand without building provider integrations or operations systems from scratch.
```

Vietnamese:

```text
Nền tảng giúp reseller mở shop VPS/proxy mang thương hiệu riêng, tự set giá, tự quản khách và ví, trong khi hệ thống xử lý catalog, order, ledger, provisioning và lifecycle phía sau.
```

## 3. Core promise

```text
Own the customer. Control the margin. Avoid the infrastructure chaos.
```

Vietnamese:

```text
Giữ khách của bạn. Tự quyết biên lợi nhuận. Không phải tự ôm hỗn loạn hạ tầng.
```

## 4. Target segments

| Segment | Pain | Message |
|---|---|---|
| Hosting reseller | manual sales, renewal chaos | launch branded VPS/proxy shop fast |
| MMO/automation community | unstable proxy/VPS source | monetize your audience with controlled storefront |
| SEO agency | scattered infrastructure for clients | centralize billing/service lifecycle |
| Freelancer/consultant | wants recurring infra revenue | sell managed infrastructure under own brand |
| Small tool shop | needs bundled infrastructure | add VPS/proxy as recurring add-on |

## 5. Non-target early segments

Avoid early:

```text
enterprise procurement-heavy customers
anonymous high-risk bulk buyers
customers demanding postpaid credit
markets requiring complex compliance not ready yet
resellers wanting abuse-heavy traffic
```

## 13. Objection handling

### “Why prepaid?”

```text
Because infrastructure cost is real and immediate. Prepaid settlement prevents fake orders, bad debt, and provider exposure. It also keeps service activation predictable.
```

### “Can I use my own domain?”

```text
Yes, white-label custom domain is part of the reseller experience depending on plan and setup readiness.
```

### “Can I add my own provider?”

```text
Provider onboarding requires technical, business, refund, and abuse evaluation. We do not add providers purely because they are cheap.
```

### “Can I get cheaper price?”

```text
Cost tiers can improve with stable volume, low abuse, and reliable payment behavior. The goal is sustainable margin, not fragile cheap inventory.
```

## 14. GTM launch sequence

```text
1. Complete private pilot.
2. Collect proof metrics.
3. Build reseller onboarding assets.
4. Invite 5–10 qualified resellers.
5. Run limited Growth offer.
6. Track reseller health and abuse.
7. Expand channels after operational stability.
```

## 15. GTM guardrails

Do not scale marketing if:

```text
- ledger reconciliation is not reliable
- provider failure modes are unclear
- support macros are missing
- abuse SOP is not ready
- tenant isolation tests fail
- provisioning idempotency is uncertain
```

## 17. Closing principle

```text
Do not sell “features.” Sell controlled recurring infrastructure revenue without operational chaos.
```
